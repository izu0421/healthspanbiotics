DESIGN

Delaying dementia progression using simple and affordable methods

INSPIRED BY NATURE

We identify probiotic strains with neuroprotective properties, inspired by naturally occurring systems and validated in lab models of disease.

Boosting brain health using AI and nature-inspired methods

Our company integrates information at different levels of biology, at the genomic, metabolomic, proteomic, cellular, and population levels. Our novel mechanistic insights into dementia lead us to identify, refine and validate a naturally-occuring probiotic bacteria that might be able to delay or prevent Alzheimer's disease pathology.

By harnessing the power of natural evolution, we developed a probiotic strain that is able to substantially improve neuronal health in pre-clinical models of Alzheimer's disease.
